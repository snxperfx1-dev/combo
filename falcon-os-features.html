02 · ArchitectureFALCON OS — Confidential
FALCON OSFractal Model
Signature Capability

Truly multi-timeframe.
The chart is just a viewport.

Most algorithms live on one chart timeframe — change the chart and the strategy changes. FALCON OS models seven absolute timeframes in parallel and lets ownership cascade down the fractal, so the result is the same on any chart.

Absolute fractal ladder Core

Structure, curves, zones and ownership are computed on W1 · D1 · H4 · H1 · M15 · M5 · M1 simultaneously — not derived from whatever chart it's attached to. The operating scale is a deliberate, tested choice, not an accident.

Ownership cascade

The highest timeframe in control "owns" price. Direction, invalidation and destination are inherited from that owning curve and escalate automatically when a higher timeframe takes over.

Per-timeframe zones

Liquidity pools, order blocks and supply/demand are built on every rung — so a trade can be priced against the zone of the timeframe that actually governs it, not just the local one.

The Curve Locator — "you are here" Novel

A single continuous coordinate of where price sits between a curve's origin and destination, anchored to the owning timeframe and never undefined — it degrades gracefully instead of resetting. The system always knows how much curve remains, so it never enters a move that is already over.

04 · Decision ContractFALCON OS — Confidential
FALCON OSCapital Discipline
Money management

Disciplined capital.
Bounded by design.

Risk control is rules and mathematics, never emotion or martingale. Three mechanisms govern every campaign.

Lock

Counter-direction lock

Never opens against a book that is currently in profit — it refuses to fight its own winning campaign, which is the single biggest source of chop in lesser systems.

Ceiling

Pre-entry basket ceiling

Position size is computed so total per-direction risk-at-stop stays under a fixed % of equity — correct at entry, no trimming after the fact.

Ladder

Live-P&L profit ladder

Banks progressively at realised reward-to-risk milestones, moves stops to protect, and rides the remainder — anchored to live positions, surviving internal resets.

Hard limits — always on
Max risk % per tradeMax lots per entry Per-direction basket ceilingDrawdown protection Session filterBreach cooldown

Risk is governed per campaign and never netted across opposing directions — each side's exposure is evaluated on its own, with a portfolio-level backstop. The system can hold long and short campaigns simultaneously on a hedging account by design.

05 · Capital DisciplineFALCON OS — Confidential
FALCON OSSelf-Learning
It learns from itself

Self-correcting.
Bounded. Auditable.

FALCON OS keeps a track record of its own decisions and adapts — within hard limits, using interpretable statistics, not a black box. Every learned value is visible on the dashboard and persisted across sessions.

Adaptive edge learning Active

Attributes every closed trade's result to the context it was taken in. Contexts that pay get sized up; contexts that persistently lose get throttled or vetoed. It learns which of its own setups have an edge — and presses them.

Regret / missed-trade learning Active

When a trade is blocked, it shadows the outcome. If a filter is repeatedly blocking trades that would have won, it learns the filter is too strict and starts taking those setups — while keeping filters that genuinely avoided losses.

Full transparency

A dedicated dashboard tab shows exactly what the system has learned — every context, its win rate, its expectancy, and its current sizing. A per-trade journal exports to CSV for independent audit.

Guardrails — a minimum sample is required before any adaptation; size multipliers are clamped; estimates are recency-weighted to track regime change; and the learning can never override direction integrity, structural invalidation, or the hard risk limits. Adaptation tunes the system; it cannot break it.
